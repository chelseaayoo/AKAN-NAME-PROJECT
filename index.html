About Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
The Akan Names from the first day to the last are :
Kwasi,Kwadwo,Kwabena,Kwaku,Yaw,Kofi,Kwame for males and,
Akosua,Adwoa,Abenaa,Akua,Yaa,Afua,Ama for females.
